Resources

There are many local and global information sources. Here are some website's of regional organizations. West Coast Seeds for examaple has an excellent guide to regional plants in British Columbia with zones, an annual calendar with recomendations on what to plant, when and where. The most outstanding nursery is Bylands Garden Centre, in West Kelowna. It's my year-round go to, especially in the Spring when its planting season. I was inspired when visiting Vancouver Island at the Horticulture Centre of the Pacific and loved the walkabout sections of gardens. The Rose, Italian, Japanese, and Sunken Gardens are spectacular at the Butchart Gardens in Saanich as well. In Kelowna, the Kasugai Japanese Gardens are an outdoor oasis in the downtown area. A historic spot with lovely blooms is the Guishigan Heritage Park For some vegetable gardening tips, you can get involved with the Central Okanagan Community Gardens where there are many neighborhoods that provide greenspace for growing legumes, herbs and root vegetables in the area.

Much of this organization is inspired by the The Kew Royal Botanical Gardens Other helpful links: Okanagan Water Basin , Botanical Gardens Niagara , iNaturalist Planning the ideal Garden
