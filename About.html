About

The Kelowna Flower Garden Club is being introduced May 1st, 2026. The founder, Silken Rauhala, has been involved in environmental organizations since completing a Bachelor of Science in Victoria, British Columbia.

Her experience includes the University of Victoria Parks Club, Trial Islands Restoration, Todd Inlet Remediation, and extensive visits to the Horticulture Centre of the Pacific and Butchart Gardens.

She has documented flora across South America, remote regions of Northern British Columbia, the Great Lakes of Ontario, and Southern California. Her goal is to bring this passion to the Okanagan and grow a community-centered garden society.
